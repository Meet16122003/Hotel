Special Undhiyu : Undhiyu is a Gujarat's very popular gujarati dish. In Undhiyu we use different vegetables with spices. Like Broad beans(papdi), sakariu, kan, bataka etc. We can serve it with thepla.

Pav Bhaji : Pav bhaji is a fast food dish from Maharashtra, consisting of a thick vegetable curry usually prepared in butter and served with a soft bread roll.

Roti Pratha : Roti pratha is a fried flour-based pancake that is cooked over a flat grill. It is usually served with a vegetable curry and is from Malaysia and Singapore. Prata is also commonly cooked upon request with cheese and potato
