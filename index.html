-----Jay Swadist Cuisine-----

Special Gujarati Thali : We offer a delightful array of dishes combining fresh vegetables and aromatic spices. Our Special Gujarati Thali includes Plain Roti/Puri, Dal, Rice, Papad, Raita, Chaas, Salad, Farsan, and more, ensuring a rich and satisfying dining experience.

Special Undhiyu : Undhiyu is a highly popular Gujarati dish from Gujarat, featuring a variety of vegetables cooked with aromatic spices. Key ingredients include Broad beans (papdi), sakariya, tender sugarcane (kan), potatoes (bataka), among others.

Pav Bhaji : Pav Bhaji is a beloved fast food dish originating from Maharashtra, known for its rich and flavorful vegetable curry cooked in butter. It is traditionally served with a soft bread roll, offering a delightful contrast of textures and a satisfying medley of spicy and savory flavors.

Roti Pratha : Roti prata is a delicious fried flour-based pancake cooked on a flat grill. Typically served with a vegetable curry, it can also be prepared upon request with cheese and potato, adding a savory twist to this popular dish.

Special Kadhi Khichdi : Kadhi Khichdi stands out as a highlight of our Gujarati thali. We serve it with buttermilk kadhi, deep-fried khichdi, along with athanu and papad. Customer satisfaction brings us great joy..

Fruit Salad and Gulab Jamun: We offer a delightful fruit salad with a creamy texture and crunchy fruits, alongside soft and sweet gulab jamun topped with sugar syrup. It's a delicious addition to our Gujarati thali, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth..
